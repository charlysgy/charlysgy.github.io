Who am I ?

I am a software engineer student at EPITA, a french engineering school. I am currently in my 2nd year of studies.

I would love to go to Dublin because it offers a unique combination of urban culture and natural beauty. The city is known for its rich history, lively pubs and delicious beer, which would allow me to indulge in one of my favorite pastimes. Additionally, the surrounding mountains offer breathtaking views and the opportunity for some refreshing walks and hikes, offering a change of pace from the bustling city. I am also drawn to the chance to discover new landscapes and immerse myself in a different culture. Overall, Dublin offers a perfect blend of city and nature, making it an ideal destination for me.
